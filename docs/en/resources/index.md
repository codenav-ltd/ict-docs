# Resources · Official Documents

> Anything claimed on this site should be cross-checked against an HKEAA or EDB document. Bookmark these.

## HKEAA — Hong Kong Examinations and Assessment Authority

| Document | Why you need it |
|----------|-----------------|
| [2026 HKDSE ICT Assessment Framework (PDF)](https://www.hkeaa.edu.hk/DocLibrary/HKDSE/Subject_Information/ict/2026hkdse-e-ict.pdf) | Definitive source for paper weights, durations, SBA |
| [2025 ICT Reference Paper](https://www.hkeaa.edu.hk/DocLibrary/HKDSE/Subject_Information/ict/2025-ICT-ref-E.pdf) | Format of reference sheet provided in Paper 1 / 2A |
| [HKEAA ICT subject page](https://www.hkeaa.edu.hk/en/hkdse/assessment/subject_information/category_a_subjects/ict/) | Latest news, sample papers, level descriptors |
| [HKDSE 2026 Examination Regulations](https://www.hkeaa.edu.hk/DocLibrary/HKDSE/Exam_Regulations/2026_HKDSE_Regulations_E.pdf) | Rules, timetables, fees |

## Past papers and sample papers

- **Past papers**: HKEAA sells official past papers. Many schools subscribe to HKEAA's online platform.
- **Sample papers**: Available on the HKEAA subject page; particularly important for **2026 onwards** because the syllabus changed.
- **Marking schemes**: Crucial — read them after every attempt to understand "what counts".
