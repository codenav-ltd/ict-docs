## Habits that compound over three years

1. **Code something every week**, even tiny вҖ” a 30-minute coding session beats a 4-hour binge.
2. **Vocabulary deck**. 5 new terms per week Г— 100 weeks = 500 terms.
3. **Past paper Sundays**. Once per month from S5 Term 2 onwards.
4. **Teach your friends**. Explaining a topic reveals what you don't really understand.

::: tip Make practice frictionless
The biggest reason students stop practising is friction вҖ” installing tools, fixing version mismatches, losing files. Use a browser-based workspace like **[SQL Books](https://sqlbooks.codenav.dev)** for SQL practice from day one, so the only thing standing between you and a `SELECT` is a tab.
:::
