## Who is this site for?

HKDSE ICT Hub is a free, open-source companion that walks alongside the official **Curriculum and Assessment Guide** issued by the **Curriculum Development Council** and **HKEAA**. It is built for:

- **S4–S6 students** who want a single source of truth covering every learning outcome that may appear in the exam.
- **ICT teachers** who need ready-made teaching plans, SBA scaffolding and trustworthy reference material in three languages.
- **Self-study candidates** preparing for the public exam without a school cohort behind them.

## What's inside

| Section | What you get |
|---------|--------------|
| [Compulsory Part](/en/compulsory/) | Module-by-module breakdown of Paper 1, including detailed sub-topics, required skills and worked examples. |
| [Electives](/en/elective/) | Side-by-side comparison plus deep dives into 2A Databases, 2B Web Application Development and 2C Algorithm & Programming. |
| [SBA Guide](/en/sba/) | Task 1 & Task 2 templates, mark scheme analysis, screenshots checklist and topic inspiration. |
| [Study Plan](/en/study-plan/) | Weekly schedules for S4, S5 and S6, plus a separate playbook for teachers. |
| [Exam Tips](/en/exam-tips/) | Paper 1 / Paper 2 time strategy, MC trap analysis and a curated list of common mistakes. |
| [Resources](/en/resources/) | Direct links to official HKEAA / EDB documents, past papers, recommended tools and a bilingual glossary. |

::: tip Practice SQL the easy way
You will need to write a **lot** of SQL for Paper 2A and your SBA. Instead of installing MySQL locally, you can practice queries on the **[SQL Books online editor](https://sqlbooks.codenav.dev)** — every account ships with its own sandbox database so you can test `SELECT`, `JOIN`, sub-queries and more without breaking anything.
:::

## How to use this site

1. **Start with the [Compulsory Part](/en/compulsory/)** to revise Paper 1 concepts.
2. **Pick your two [electives](/en/elective/)** and follow the dedicated chapters.
3. **Bookmark the [SBA guide](/en/sba/)** while you build your Task 1 system.
4. **Run mock exams** using past papers from the [Resources](/en/resources/) page.
5. **Use the language switcher** (top-right) any time you want to flip between English, 繁體中文 and 简体中文.
